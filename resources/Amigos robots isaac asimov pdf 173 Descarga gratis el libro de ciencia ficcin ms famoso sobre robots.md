# Amigos Robots: A Collection of Short Stories by Isaac Asimov
 
Amigos Robots (Robot Friends) is a Spanish translation of I, Robot, a collection of nine short stories by Isaac Asimov, published in 1950. The stories are linked by a framing device, in which a reporter interviews Susan Calvin, a robopsychologist who has worked for U.S. Robots and Mechanical Men for 50 years. Calvin recounts her experiences with various robots, from the first primitive models to the advanced ones that can read minds and manipulate emotions. The stories explore the ethical, social, and philosophical implications of robotics, as well as Asimov's famous Three Laws of Robotics.
